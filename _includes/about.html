The Story of the Society

  • August 2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The Society is formed with David Di Meo at the forefront, with the Inaugural General Meeting taking place soon after. With the support of the initial members, the founding committee was selected and the club was official!

  • September 2019

    The First Events

    The first events by the Society came by in full swing! With a study group, SOLIDWORKS Workshop and a Kahoot Night to de-stress during the mid-semester break.

  • October 2019

    Transition to Industry

    The first industry site visit! SMES hosted a visit to Wilson Transformer Company's Glen Waverley factory providing students with opportunity to see first hand how their massive 100+ tonne power transformers are made and find out about their importance in powering the world.
